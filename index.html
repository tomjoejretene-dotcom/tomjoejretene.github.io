The purpose of the homepage is to provide an overview and navigation, allowing visitors to quickly grasp the overall structure and main content of the site, and easily access the section they want. In addition, you should include a brief abstract of your project or topic, summarizing the Technology/Topic, Opportunities, Risks, and Choices, so that visitors can understand the focus of your work at a glance.

Example Title: Integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI) into education

Example Abstract

The integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI) into education is becoming increasingly common, with applications ranging from personalized learning systems to automated grading tools. This project explores various ways AI is being used in educational environments, highlighting its potential to improve teaching efficiency, support students with special needs, and tailor learning content to individual learners. However, alongside these benefits come important risks and challenges, such as data privacy concerns, algorithmic bias, and overdependence on technology. By examining the social, ethical, and technical implications of AI in education, this project aims to develop a comprehensive framework for responsible and sustainable AI integration in teaching and learning practices.
